Jungroo uses artificial intelligence to generate insights that help students learn, teachers teach and leaders lead.

Student

Jungroo AI bot identifies micro level mastery of student and creates personalized learning path for every student

Teacher

Jungroo AI bot generates customized teaching plans and groups kids based on learning level insights of each and every student

School

Year long monitoring and evaluation using learning levels data of all students

Faster Diagnosis

Jungroo uses Reinforcement learning algorithms to find the learning levels of the student in with minimum possible questions and minimum possible time

Automatic Grouping

Jungroo uses Clustering algorithms to group the students based on their concept mastery and enabling teachers to give optimum targeted interventions to groups.

Customized Teaching Plan

Jungroo recommends teachers with customized teaching plans for the classroom based on the percentage concept mastery of all students and time.

Continuous Monitoring

Jungroo helps teachers by tracking student's performance and Principals by tracking classroom's performance across the acadamic year.
